What We Stand For

Fairness

Algorithms should benefit everyone equally, not reinforce bias.

Transparency

Users have the right to know when and how their data is analysed.

Consent

Data collection should always have a clear reason and permission.

Accountability

Organisations must take resposbility for their models.

Security

Protecting data is protecting people
