Data is everywhere, powering healthcare, transport, finance, and daily life. But behind every algorithm sits a question: is this fair, transparent, and safe?

What We Stand For

Fairness
Algorithms should benefit everyone equally, not reinforce bias.
Transparency
Users have the right to know when and how their data is analysed.
Consent
Data collection should always have a clear reason and permission.
Accountability
Organisations must take responsibility for their models.
Security
Protecting data is protecting people.
